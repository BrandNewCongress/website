We believe that, we,

The American People

  • Have the potential to overcome our greatest problems

    We have 320 million amazing people, vast natural resources, the best universities and research centers in the world, and an economy still rich with capital and innovation despite 40 years of economic decline.

  • Can rebuild our communities, economy and schools.

    We believe America is finally poised to ensure true liberty and justice for all by:

    • guaranteeing quality education, healthcare and childcare
    • reversing mass incarceration
    • reforming our entire justice system

  • Can reclaim our government.

    We believe we can restore our democracy by removing the corrupting influence of money from politics.


OUR BRAND NEW CONGRESS WILL

Restart America's Economic Engine

We will invest in high-wage, high-growth industries instead of the Wall Street casino, creating unprecedented prosperity and opportunity for all Americans.


For Families

  • We will fully fund early education in every zip code.
  • Make high quality child care available to all families who need it.
  • Provide every child a top-quality education, no matter where they live.
  • Eliminate the burden of tuition at all public universities, colleges and trade schools for students and their families.
  • Make the minimum wage a living wage and tie it to inflation.
  • Guarantee family leave for all workers.
  • Cut taxes for low and middle income Americans. Simplify tax laws and eliminate filing for most Americans (since the IRS already records earnings).

For Communities

  • We will fix broken infrastructure with a $4.6 trillion investment
  • Build community centers, remove crumbling buildings, renovate downtowns, repair county/municipal infrastructure, and much more through local grants.

For Our Country & World

  • We will build a 100% renewable energy economy in 10 years.



OUR BRAND NEW CONGRESS WILL

ProTECT LIBERTY AND JUSTICE FOR ALL

When hard-working Americans cannot escape poverty or prison, the system is rigged. When a nurse’s vote is worth less than a billionaire’s vote, the system is rigged. We will can and will change:


Health Care

  • Medicare for all! Every American will be covered by a single-payer system. Insurance companies will compete to offer supplemental plans in a truly free market .

GENDER DISCRIMINATION

  • We will close the pay gap for women and bring millions of women out of poverty.
  • Include reproductive health care as part of a Medicare For All system.
  • Protect LGBT+ lives and rights by enacting common sense legislation that ensures people of all sexuality and identity enjoy the same rights.

IMMIGRATION

  • Our comprehensive immigration reform will recognize that:
    • The United States has always been driven by immigrants
    • Our society and economy are made stronger by immigrants
    • Today we are suffering from a decline in immigration

  • We will focus on inclusion instead of isolation.

MINORITY OPPRESSION

  • We will enact sweeping criminal justice reform to end systemic oppression of African Americans, Latinos, Native Americans, and low-income Americans.
  • End the school-to-prison pipeline.
  • Prosecute law enforcement members for acts of criminal violence.

PRISON

  • We will reverse mass incarceration through government focus on reducing prison populations, which often leads to a fall in crime rates.
  • End the “war on drugs” that has cost millions of lives and wasted trillions of dollars
  • End all private prisons and make it illegal for any industry to profit from prison labor. .
  • Invest in programs to reintegrate former prisoners into communities and help them find jobs in the new, high-growth economy.
  • Call upon millions of Americans to help restore the status of returning citizens.

ELECTIONS

  • We will remove money from politics.
  • Eliminate the electoral college.
  • Investigating alternative voting systems such as ranked choice voting. .
  • Create a national election holiday.
  • Improve voting mechanics to make voting easy and fraud impossible.

WARS OF CHOICE

  • We will stop destabilizing other countries and focus on fixing our own.
